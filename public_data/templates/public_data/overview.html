Open Humans Public Data Sharing

Overview of Feature Activation

Activating this feature will allow you to publicly share data sets you have imported to Open Humans, turning it into a public resource available to researchers, citizen scientists, and any others that may wish to learn from it and build upon it.

Requirements

To activate this feature, you must:

  • be at least 18 years of age
    Sharing data publicly could involve risks. To make this decision, we require you to be at least 18 years of age.
  • be representing yourself
    This decision must be made by the individual themselves. A parent or legal guardian may not choose to publicly share data from a child or adult in their care.
  • read the information provided
    The information we provide here is designed to be informative and readable. It is very important that you take the time to read this.
  • pass our quiz
    You must complete a brief quiz to demonstrate your understanding of this feature. This quiz should take only a few minutes of your time, and helps assure us that you understand potential risks.
