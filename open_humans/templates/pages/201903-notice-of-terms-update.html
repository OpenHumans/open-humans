March 2019 Updates to our terms & policies

Open Humans strives to be a safe and transparent environment for you to receive, manage, and share your personal data. From time to time, we review our terms and policies and determine that updates are desired, to clarify or improve them.

Old text:

New text:
Open Humans is operated within the United States and complies with all applicable US laws, as well as the European Union General Data Protection Regulation. You can read more about this on our Records of Personal Data Processing Activities report.
