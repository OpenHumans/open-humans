Public data API

You don't need to set up a study to work with our public data! Get started right away with data sets some Open Humans members have generously chosen to share publicly!

Data is publicly listed on members' public profiles, and is also available through our Public Data API. It's very easy to use – because the API only returns public data, no authentication is required!

Users of the API are expected to follow our guidelines for using public data.

Connect your study

Open Humans believes researchers that work with our members should also be committed to building the commons: we want to see research studies return data to participants. By doing this, research partners commit to transparency and equity, and their participants can use and share the resulting data.

We want to support researchers interested in giving raw data access to their participants. Reasons you might want to do this include:

  • see your study highlighted by Open Humans
  • make your participants valued partners in research
  • pursue new methods of data sharing
  • create a path to public data, shared by some participants
  • see your work cited when other studies use your data
Ask us about connecting studies at support@openhumans.org
